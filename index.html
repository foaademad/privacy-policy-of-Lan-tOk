Welcome to Lan Tok. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

By using Lan Tok, you agree to the information collection and use practices described in this policy.

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

7. Cookies

We use cookies to track your activity and improve your experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings, but this may affect the functionality of the application.

8. Children's Privacy

Lan Tok is intended for users who are 13 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age. If we learn that we have collected information from a child under 13, we will delete that information immediately.

9. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes through notifications in the app or via email. We advise you to review this policy regularly.
